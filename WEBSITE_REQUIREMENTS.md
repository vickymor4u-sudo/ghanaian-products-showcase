# Ghanaian Export Website Strategy - Requirements

## Purpose
B2B export showcase designed to build credibility and attract international distributors, importers, wholesalers, and agents. NOT a consumer retail shop.

## Core Website Structure

### 1. Homepage
- Communicates who you are and what you export within 10-15 seconds
- Clear headline, export focus, regions served
- Strong CTAs: "Request Product Catalog" or "Become a Distributor"

### 2. Products Page
- Dedicated sections for each brand:
  - Fufu Borga
  - Gari Borga
  - Kokonte Borga
  - Banku Borga
- Each includes:
  - Product overview
  - Variants
  - Packaging options
  - Shelf life
  - Ingredients
  - Origin
  - MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)
  - OEM/Private label availability

### 3. Export & Compliance
- Export destinations
- Compliance readiness (FDA, labeling, shelf life)
- Customization options
- Logistics details (FOB/CIF terms, port of origin, lead times)

### 4. About Us
- Present Supply & Demand Worldwide Ltd as professional export-focused company
- Emphasize: value-chain role, Ghana sourcing/production, China-based market presence

### 5. Why Partner With Us
- Competitive advantages:
  - Standardized quality
  - Export experience
  - Flexible MOQs
  - Transparent communication
  - Long-term partnership mindset

### 6. Media & Downloads
- Product photos
- Packaging mockups
- Downloadable catalogs
- Supporting documents for buyer presentations

### 7. Contact & Inquiry
- Structured inquiry form to qualify leads:
  - Company name
  - Country
  - Intended order volume
  - Product interest
- Business email contact
- WhatsApp contact

## Design Principles
- Clean, minimal, professional, export-focused
- Avoid: overly colorful designs, heavy animations, cluttered layouts, consumer-style CTAs
- Visual feel: trade fair or corporate export presentation
- Tone: confident, precise, business-oriented
- Avoid exaggerated claims or vague marketing buzzwords

## Language & Positioning
- Primary language: English
- Tone: confident, precise, business-oriented
- Avoid exaggerated claims or vague marketing buzzwords

## Technical Requirements
- Mobile responsive
- Fast loading speed
- Basic SEO for Ghana export keywords
- Inquiry forms with analytics
- Reliable hosting
- Advanced e-commerce can be added later if needed

## Strategic Cautions
- NOT designed for local consumer retail
- NOT a simple marketing brochure
- Do NOT hide commercial realities (MOQ, export terms)
- Do NOT overpromise certifications or capabilities
- Maintain trust through transparency
